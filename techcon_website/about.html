History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 50 passionate technologists in a modest conference room in Silicon Valley. Founded by a group of visionary entrepreneurs who believed in the power of knowledge sharing, the conference was born from a simple idea: bringing together brilliant minds to discuss the future of technology.

Over the past 14 years, TechCon has evolved into one of the world's premier technology conferences, attracting over 10,000 attendees annually from more than 60 countries. What started as discussions about mobile computing and social media has expanded to encompass artificial intelligence, quantum computing, blockchain technology, and sustainable tech solutions.

Each year has brought new innovations and breakthrough moments. In 2015, we witnessed the first live demonstration of autonomous vehicle technology. In 2018, we hosted the largest gathering of AI researchers outside of academic institutions. By 2022, TechCon became carbon-neutral, leading the industry in sustainable conference practices.

Mission

Our mission at TechCon is to accelerate technological innovation by fostering collaboration, education, and inspiration among the global tech community. We believe that the most groundbreaking solutions emerge when diverse minds come together to share knowledge, challenge assumptions, and envision the future.

We are committed to democratizing access to cutting-edge technology insights, ensuring that both seasoned professionals and emerging talents have equal opportunities to learn, network, and contribute to the technological advancement of our world. TechCon serves as a catalyst for meaningful connections that drive industry progress.

Our core principles include promoting ethical technology development, supporting underrepresented voices in tech, and addressing the global challenges through innovative solutions. We envision a future where technology serves humanity's greatest needs while preserving our planet for future generations.

Past Speakers

TechCon has been honored to host some of the most influential figures in technology, each bringing unique perspectives and groundbreaking insights to our community.

Dr. Sarah Chen

Chief AI Officer at FutureTech Industries and pioneer in machine learning ethics. Dr. Chen's 2023 keynote on "Responsible AI Development" has been viewed over 2 million times and influenced policy discussions worldwide. She holds a Ph.D. in Computer Science from MIT and has authored over 100 research papers on artificial intelligence.

Marcus Rodriguez

Founder and CEO of GreenTech Solutions, a company revolutionizing renewable energy through smart grid technology. His 2022 presentation on "The Internet of Energy" demonstrated how blockchain and IoT can transform power distribution. Under his leadership, GreenTech has reduced energy waste by 40% in over 200 smart cities globally.

Dr. Amara Okonkwo

Quantum Computing Research Director at Quantum Leap Labs and TED Speaker. Dr. Okonkwo's groundbreaking work in quantum cryptography has earned her recognition as one of Time Magazine's "100 Most Influential People." Her 2021 TechCon presentation "Quantum Computing for Everyone" made complex quantum concepts accessible to thousands of developers worldwide.

James Park

Co-founder of CloudScale Dynamics and cybersecurity expert. Park's innovative approach to zero-trust architecture has protected over 500 Fortune 500 companies from cyber threats. His 2020 virtual presentation during the pandemic on "Securing Remote Work" became the blueprint for countless organizations transitioning to distributed teams.
